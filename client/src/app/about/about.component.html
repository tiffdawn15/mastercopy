Explore More Art History...

MasterCopy is a work of love created by Tiffany Messer to provide museum catalogues to everyone. Investigate works in art history movements; your favorite artist; or just click around the catalogue. All artworks are provided by the Chicago Institute of Art’s open source api and all information belongs to them.

Hi, I'm Tiff!

I’m a software engineer and a self taught artistes. I’m on my self taught artist journey and I am currently doing a lot of master copies in order to create my own unique voice. Mastercopy is an effort that allows me to have access to more quality images and to share my love of art history with anyone interested.
