MasterCopy

MasterCopy is a work of love created by Tiffany Messer a self taught artist and software engineer. She has been dedicated to her art practice since the pandemic and patient about expanding her artist voice through the use of creating master copies of other artist work. After many months of struggling to find good reference photos online, she discovered that Chicago Institute of Art provides and open source API. MasterCopy is a Pinterest clode using CIA’s api. MasterCopy is currently in its infancy. If you art interested in the project please reach out to Tiffany and let her know. She has a lot of ideas about ways to improve the website, and would love to hear how the site works for others.
